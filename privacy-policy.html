PRIVACY POLICY

OVERVIEW

FOUR is a minimalist card game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

DATA WE COLLECT

FOUR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All game data — including your gold balance, best streak, purchased themes, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES

FOUR does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. No data is shared with third parties.

PERMISSIONS

FOUR does not request any sensitive device permissions such as camera, microphone, location, or contacts. The app only uses local storage to save your game progress.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

FOUR does not collect any data from users of any age. The app is safe for all audiences.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this privacy policy changes in future versions of FOUR, the updated policy will be published at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
